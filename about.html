ABOUT ME

My name is Patrick Caron; I am a driven and creative front-end developer with over two years of hands-on experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery and Adobe Photoshop--websites are my thing. I'm always eager to learn what's on the bleeding edge of the Web-Dev world and I love to work with people who share that desire.

With that said, it takes communication to get the most out of working with talented and energetic people. I have a strong background of working with and understanding clients, designers, team members, and management. I am also a very patient and attentive person and I enjoy both teaching and learning from my team.

Ultimately, the reason I am so passionate about web development is that it allows me meld logical thinking with creative expression, and that fusion results in an eclectic experience that I will never grow tired of.
